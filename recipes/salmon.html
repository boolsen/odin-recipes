Description

Salmon is a healthy alternative to the usual meaty dish. Try to be healthy for once you bloodmuncher

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds Salmon
  • 1 egg
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup dried bread crumbs
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons prepared mustard
  • 1/3 cup ketchup

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. Beat egg in a medium bowl. Mix in onion, milk, and bread crumbs. Place salmon in the baking dish. Season salmon with salt and pepper, and spread with the egg mixture.
  3. In a small bowl, mix the brown sugar, mustard, and ketchup. Pour over the salmon.
  4. Bake 1 hour in the preheated oven, or until the sauce is bubbly and salmon flakes easily with a fork.
